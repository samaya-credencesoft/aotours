Tour Description

See two world-famous visitor attractions in north island all in one wonderful day. Visit Waitomo Glowworm Caves and continue to the Hobbiton Movie Set for a tour of one of the most beautiful movie sets worldwide, made famous by the movie trilogies “The Lord of the Rings” and “The Hobbit”.

Additional Info

  • Pick Up TimeCommence Auckland with hotel pickups 7.30 am to 8.30 am.
  • Drop Off TimeBetween 7.30 pm to 8.30 pm.

Special Note :

  • * Refunds based on the refund of our service provider.

  • * On the occasion of Minimum Pax not met, tour won’t commence.

  • * Lunch is not included in price.

1

On the way to Waitomo we pass through beautiful countryside of the Waikato and King Country approximately 2.5 hour journey from Auckland to the Waitomo Glowworm Caves.

2

At Waitomo, you will experience the world-famous Glowworm Caves on a 45-minute tour, which includes a boat ride on an underground stream to view the magically dazzling Glowworms.

3

We continue on to the Hobbiton Movie Set near the rural town of Matamata, made world-famous as the original setting of Peter Jackson’s “The Lord of the Rings”- and “The Hobbit”- trilogies. Now you are able to see the Hobbiton Movie Set during a 2-hour tour, giving you astonishing insights into how these movies were made and which parts of the movies were filmed here.
