1

In Auckland City and Devonport :

Bird’s eye view from Sky tower observation Deck, drive through the Auckland Domain where the beautiful Auckland Museum is located and have time to stop to marvel at Auckland’s 2nd-largest volcano.

View several ancient volcanoes during the Auckland Tour, noting Auckland is built on a volcanic field of 55 volcanoes.

Continue with a drive through the boutique suburb of Parnell, restored in the old colonial style and with many beautiful mansions.

Drive along Tamaki Drive waterfront, one of the world’s most beautiful city drives, you will see several of the stunning beaches along Auckland's shoreline.

Pass through Mission Bay, arguably Auckland’s most well-known beach with lovely golden sand.

Drive over the famous Auckland Harbour Bridge, spanning sparkling blue waters dotted with white sails.

Arrive in Devonport, the picturesque old North Shore marine village nestled in the shadow of historic Mount Victoria with great cafes and boutiques and blessed with a wonderful view back across to Auckland city.

Enjoy great views of the famous Hauraki Gulf and the mystical island volcano Rangitoto Island, Auckland’s largest and at 600 years also youngest volcano.

Visit Mount Eden Summit and enjoy looking at the sacred crater. There is different kind of energy and some magnificent views of the city all around.

Visit 670 Acres expansive parkland in the heart of Auckland, surrounding a volcanic peak, the hill of One Tree Hill-the important memorial place for both Maori and other New Zealanders.

2

Auckland's West Coast and Waitakere Ranges

Drive through Titirangi (Maori for Fringe of Heaven), the artists’ enclave on the Manukau Harbour.

Visit the Arataki Visitor Centre at the gateway to Auckland's famous West Coast with its very interesting displays and magnificent views over the Manukau Harbour.

Spectacular ocean vistas along Auckland’s wild West Coast in our famous Waitakere Ranges; Crystal Mountain, having NZ’s largest collection of Crystals and Minerals.

We visit the most stunning of all the black sand beaches on Auckland's famous West Coast.

Places To Cover

Auckland City , Sky Tower, Auckland Museum, Mission Bay beach, Devenport, Mount Eden Crater, Cornwall Park,Waitakere Ranges & Crystal Mountain .
