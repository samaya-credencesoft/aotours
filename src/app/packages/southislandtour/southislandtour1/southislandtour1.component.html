Tour Description

Day Travel Highlights Night Stay
Day  1(D) Arrive in Queenstown anytime in the afternoon. Visit Frankton Lake, Glenorchy and rest of the evening is free and easy- to walk around Queenstown Lake Wakatipu waterfront, surrounding areas and browse through the shops. Dinner at local Indian restaurant. Queenstown
Day  2(B,D) Have a day trip to see some of the best sceneries New Zealand has to offer. The day trip to Milford Sound is through fascinating scenery of Milford Road – plains, tunnel, snow mountains and river valleys. Upon reaching Milford Sound, cruise through the fiords in the middle of mighty glacier carved mountains. Picnic lunch will be served on the cruise. Return to Queenstown same day. Queenstown
Day  3(B) Check out of accommodation. Visit various attractions of Queenstown including the Gondola Luge, Lake Hayes, Shotover Jet Boat site , Arrowtown, A J Hackett Bungy Centre, Wanaka- Puzzling World, Glendhu bay, Wanaka tree etc. Depart from Queenstown anytime after 6.00 pm. -
