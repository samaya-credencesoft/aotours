Tour Description

Day Travel Highlights Night Stay
Day  1(D) Arrive in Queenstown anytime in the afternoon. Visit Frankton Lake, Glenorchy and rest of the evening is free and easy- to walk around Queenstown Lake Wakatipu waterfront and browse through the shops in the surrounding areas. Dinner at local Indian restaurant. . Dinner at local Indian restaurant. Queenstown
Day  2(B) Have a day trip to see some of the best sceneries New Zealand has to offer. The day trip to Milford Sound is through the fascinating scenery of Milford Road – plains, tunnel, snow mountains and river valleys. Upon reaching Milford Sound, cruise through the fiords in the middle of mighty glacier carved mountains. Picnic lunch will be served on the cruise. Return to Queenstown same day. Queenstown
Day  3(B) Morning visit the various attractions of Queenstown- Glenorchy, Gondola Luge, Arrowtown, Wanaka, Puzzling World at Wanaka, Wanaka tree, Glendhu bay. Queenstown
Day  4(B,D) Depart from Queenstown and travel along Kawarau gorge and Lindis Pass to Mount Cook. See Mount Cook National Park. See the snow covered tallest peak in Australasia – Mt. Cook.Enjoy a 4WD tour to see glacial lakes with floating icebergs. Option to upgrade to Mt. Cook Ski Plane / Glacier Explorer boat ride (summer). Stay 1 night in Mt Cook.
Check in accommodation. Stay 1 night in Christchurch. 		Mt. Cook
Day  5(B) Travel to Tekapo. Visit the highlights of Tekapo – Lake Tekapo, Church of Good shepherd and the sheep dog statue. Continue travel through Canterbury plains to reach Christchurch. Afternoon tour around Christchurch – Avon river, Botanic Garden, Cathedral Square, Hagley Oval. Depart Christchurch and drop off at airport 6.30pm or later. -
