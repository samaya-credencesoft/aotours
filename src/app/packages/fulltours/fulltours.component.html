Tour Description

Day Travel Highlights Night Stay
Day  1 Arrive in Auckland by 1 pm or earlier. Pickup rental car in airport and drive through back country roads to Rotorua. In the afternoon visit-Rotorua Museum & Government Gardens and Kuirau park. Rotorua
Day  2 visit local attractions of Rotorua - Morning visiting tour of Waimangu Valley, in the afternoon Agrodome Sheep shearing Show and in the evening experience the geothermal region-Te-Puia -Maori village to see geysers (erupting every hour) and boiling mud pools. Rotorua
Day  3 Morning visit- Redwood forest, Blue & Green Lake and travel through back country roads to Waitomo Caves. Enjoy a guided tour of Waitomo Glowworm Caves. Continue travel to Hamilton- (Visit Hamilton Gardens) and proceed to Auckland. Early arrival in Auckland- is free and easy for shopping. Auckland
Day  4 Check out of accommodation. Morning visit the highlights of Auckland including Auckland Museum, Sky tower, Mission Bay beach and Mt. Eden volcano crater. Auckland
Day  5 Depart from Auckland early morning. Travel north to Bay of Islands. Take the Hole in the Rock cruise, chances to see dolphins. Evening viewing tour of scenic Waitangi Treaty Grounds- considered as the most historic place of New Zealand and then have a relaxed time near the wharf. Check into accommodation. Bay of Islands
Day  6 Morning check out of accommodation, join and enjoy a fun filled day tour of Cape Reinga and 90 Mile beach guided by the locals. Visits include Ancient Kauri Kingdom, Wagener Park, Rarawa beach, Cape Reinga, Te Paki Sand Dunes, 90 Mile Beach, Aupouri forest & nursery. Return to Paihia about 6.30 pm Bay of Islands
Day  7 Morning- continue travel to Auckland airport via Whangarai. Return rental car at the airport and 2 pm or later fly Auckland to Queenstown .Pickup rental car at Queenstown airport. Stay 3 nights in Queenstown rest of the evening is free and easy- to walk around Queenstown Lake Wakatipu. Queenstown
Day  8 Have a day trip to see some of the best sceneries New Zealand has to offer. The day trip to Milford Sound is through fascinating scenery of Milford Road – plains, tunnel, snow mountains and river Upon reaching Milford Sound, cruise through the fiords in the middle of mighty glacier carved mountains. Picnic lunch will be served on the cruise. Return to Queenstown same day. Queenstown
Day  9 Check out of accommodation. Visit various attraction of Queenstown-Frankton Lake, Glenorchy, Gondola Luge, Arrowtown, Wanaka, Puzzling World at Wanaka, Wanaka tree, Glendhu bay. Queenstown
Day  10 Depart from Queenstown and travel along Kawarau gorge and Lindis Pass to Mount Cook. Visit Mount Cook National Park. See the snow covered tallest peak in Australasia – Mt. Cook. Enjoy a 4WD tour to see glacial lakes with floating icebergs. Option to upgrade to Mt. Cook Ski Plane / Glacier Explorer boat ride (summer). Stay 1 night in Mt Cook. Mt. Cook
Day  11 Morning visit the highlights of Tekapo – Lake Tekapo, Church of Good shepherd and the sheep dog statue. Continue travel to Christchurch. Christchurch
Day  12 Morning at your own pace visit the highlights of Christchurch including Botanic gardens, Avon river Cathedral Square and Hagley Oval. Afternoon drop off at airport. Depart from Christchurch 2.00 pm or later. -
