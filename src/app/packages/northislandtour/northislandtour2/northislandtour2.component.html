Tour Description

Day Travel Highlights Night Stay
Day  1(B) Visit the thermal wonderland Rotorua and Waitomo. Morning enjoy a guided tour of Waitomo Glowworm Caves. Afternoon travel to Rotorua. Reached Rotorua in the evening and visit-Rotorua Museum, Government Gardens Kuirau park. Rotorua
Day  2(B) Morning visit Te Puia Maori village to see geysers (erupting every hour) boiling mud pools and Maori culture show then visit local attractions of Rotorua- Agrodome Sheep shearing Show, Gondola luge, Redwood Forest, Blue & Green Lake. Rotorua
Day  3(B) Visit Waimangu Valley ( Lake Rotomahana Cruise) and depart Scenic Road travel to Taupo- Visit the highlights of Taupo including the gushing Huka Falls, large Lake Taupo, Taupo bungy site and geothermal valley and back to Rotorua. Rotorua
Day  4(B) Check out of accommodation. Depart Rotorua with the option to visit Hamilton Gardens and viewing tour of Hobbiton Movie Set near the rural town of Matamata, made world-famous as the original setting of Peter Jackson’s “The Lord of the Rings”- and “The Hobbit”- trilogies. -
