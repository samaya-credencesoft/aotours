Tour Description

Day Travel Highlights Night Stay
Day  1 Depart from Auckland early morning. Travel north via Whangarei to Paihia, Bay of Islands. Take the Hole in the Rock cruise around the Bay of Islands, chances to see dolphins. Option to visit the scenic Waitangi Treaty Grounds, considered as the most historic place of New Zealand. Have a relaxed time near the wharf. Play in the beaches or browse through the shops. – Check into accommodation. Stay 1 night in Paihia. Paihia
Day  2 Check out of accommodation. Join the Cape Reinga tour starting at early morning. Enjoy a fun filled day tour of Cape Reinga and 90 Mile beach, guided by the locals. Visits include Ancient Kauri Kingdom, Wagener Park, Rarawa beach, Cape Reinga, Tapotupotu Bay, Te Paki Sand Dunes (toboggans on bus), 90 Mile Beach, Aupouri forest & nursery. Return to Paihia about 6.30 pm. Paihia
Day  3 Free morning time in Paihia today. You might like to take the ferry across to the beautiful township of Russell – NZ’s first capital (extra cost) explores Russell at your own pace and returns to Paihia wharf. or just relax in any one of the cafes. Depart Paihia with the option to visit Waipoua kauri forest region and view giant kauri trees including Tane Mahuta which is aged more than 1000 Years old and your journey back to Auckland. -
